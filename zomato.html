NON-VEG

                    

Non-vegetarian meals typically include meat, poultry, fish, or seafood as their main components, providing rich sources of protein, essential vitamins, and minerals. These meals are diverse, ranging from simple dishes like grilled chicken to complex cuisines like seafood paella or beef stew. They can be prepared using various cooking methods, including grilling, roasting, frying, or steaming.


VEG

                    

Vegetarian meals focus on plant-based ingredients such as vegetables, fruits, grains, legumes, nuts, and seeds, offering a wide range of nutrients and flavors. They can include dishes like vegetable stir-fries, lentil soups, and salads, rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants. These meals often utilize herbs and spices to create vibrant and aromatic flavors without relying on meat.
